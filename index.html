Our Story

Built on Precision.
Defined by Culture.

Babylon Barbers is a premium barbershop in the heart of Cherry Hill, NJ. Founded by Mohammed — an engineer-turned-barber who's been behind the chair since age 15 — we bring obsessive attention to detail and a love for the craft to every single cut.

Every haircut includes a refreshing wash, blow-dry, and style. Beard services come with hot towels, premium shaving gel, and precision razor work.
